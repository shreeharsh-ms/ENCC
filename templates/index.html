About The Event

The core of your event, the hackathon, is a competition where participants, often tech-savvy individuals, work together or individually to create innovative software solutions, often within a limited timeframe. It's a platform for coding enthusiasts to put their skills to the test and develop new, practical applications.

Sponsors

Domains

Artificial Intelligence

Dive into the world of intelligent systems and machine learning! Tackle challenges in computer vision, natural language processing, predictive modeling, and more. Bring AI solutions to life and showcase your innovation in automation, decision-making, and data analysis.

Cybersecurity

Test your skills in defending against digital threats! Solve real-world cybersecurity challenges by identifying vulnerabilities, preventing attacks, and securing systems. This is your chance to show expertise in protecting networks, data, and users from evolving cyber threats.

Blockchain

Explore the future of decentralized technology! Develop blockchain-based solutions for security, transparency, and efficiency. From creating smart contracts to building dApps, this domain offers a platform to revolutionize industries with blockchain innovation.

Web Development

Push the limits of modern web technologies! Build innovative, responsive, and interactive websites or applications. Whether it’s frontend or backend, showcase your skills in creating dynamic, user-friendly web experiences using the latest frameworks and tools.

Cloud Computing

Harness the power of the cloud! Build scalable, secure, and efficient applications using cloud platforms. Whether it’s infrastructure as a service (IaaS), platform as a service (PaaS), or software as a service (SaaS), create solutions that leverage the cloud’s capabilities for businesses and users.
